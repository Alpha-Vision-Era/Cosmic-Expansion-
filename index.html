The Fluidic Visualization of Galactic Expansion

This work introduces a symbolic but scientifically resonant model of galactic motion. Drawing from the analogy of a punctured water bag, it reimagines Sagittarius A*, the Milky Way’s central black hole, as a gravitational rupture from which structured cosmic expansion flows. The model bridges physical cosmology with metaphysical interpretation, illustrating the universe’s motion as a divine act of ordered release.
